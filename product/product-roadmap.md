# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Staff Directory
Manage employee profiles, roles, contact details, and documents.

### 2. Attendance & Holidays
Track daily presence, manage absences, and configure recurring holiday rules.

### 3. Payroll & Finance
Handle salary calculation, advances, bonuses, and payment history.

### 4. Settings & Access
User registration, household management, and member invitations.

### 5. Onboarding & Setup
Guide new users through the initial configuration of their household and staff.

### 6. Employee Portal
Public-facing view for staff to check their own records via phone number.
